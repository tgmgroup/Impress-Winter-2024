Hello, this is George, and thanks for reading this Winter, 2024 issue of Impress. The theme of this issue is "Season's Greetings." It's a common greeting (あいさつ) in winter because of the Christmas season. This is the last season of the year, and because it's the last season of our school year, too, this issue's quote is about memories.

"Seasons come and go, but the memories of each season stay with us forever."

Some of you will be leaving Isesaki next year for greater things, so we'd just like you to know that we enjoyed our time with you and we will always remember our time together. So do your best, and make us proud.

Enjoy the magazine.


P.S. Sorry for the lateness of the issue!
